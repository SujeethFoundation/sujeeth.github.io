About Us

the bullets

Who We Are?

The Sujeeth Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to unlocking the opportunity of education.

What We Do?

We help students network into their first job. We partner students with a mentor for a six-week program that encourages students to exercise agency in their career.

Why We Do It?

Academic institutions are a place where experimentation thrives and opportunities to grow are abundant. We want to unlock that value for every student.

Since When?

We launched on May 2023. We are currently partnered with the University of Missouri.

Programs Portal

Career circle embeds concepts from leading thought leaders. To enrich the experience, we provide students access to a curated list of Ted Talks. In addition, we will provide the books written by these thought leaders to students for free.

The Programs Portal provides a vehicle for students to learn more about what we offer. Students can request books through the portal.
