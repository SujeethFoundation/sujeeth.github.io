About Us

the bullets

Who We Are?

The Sujeeth Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to unlocking the opportunity of education.

What We Do?

We help students network into their first job. We teach students about agency. We support students through Work Out Loud Circles.

Why We Do It?

Academic institutions are a place where experimentation thrives and opportunities to grow are abundant. We want to unlock that value for every student.

Since When?

We launched on May 2023. This year we are establishing our first University partnership.

Programs Portal

Education isn't just about textbooks; it's about instilling resilience, nurturing growth mindsets, and honing effective communication skills. We provide students with invaluable resources, from Angela Duckworth's "Grit" to Carol Dweck's "Mindset," and John Stepper's "Work Out Loud." But our commitment doesn't end there. Through engaging "Work Out Loud" circles, we create safe spaces for students to practice these skills, fostering a hands-on learning experience.

Equip Yourself

Books

Request free copies of Angela Duckworth's "Grit," Carol Dweck's "Mindset," and John Stepper's "Work Out Loud." Tap into a wealth of wisdom and techniques that will empower you to overcome challenges, develop a positive mindset, and excel in teamwork.

Circles

Participate in our "Work Out Loud" circles to transform theory into practice, enhancing communication, collaboration, and the application of key concepts from books like "Grit," "Mindset," and "Work Out Loud" for real-world success.
